The Temple of Bel was the religious center of Palmyra and it was dedicated in 32 AD. The gods Bel, Aglibol and Yarhibol were worshiped in this temple as a triad. This is a photogrammetric model of a stone relief ceiling that stood inside the Temple of Bel.
