The model is a relief of from the Palace of Ashurnasirpal II located in Nimrud. The original relief was carved in alabaster and represents a winged protective spirit with a text inscribed in it. The textures are composed with images provided from Jim Peak and Staff Sgt. JoAnn Makinano.
